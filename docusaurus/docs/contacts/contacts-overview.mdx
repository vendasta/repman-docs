## What is the Contacts Tab?

The Contacts tab in Reputation Management displays a list of all saved contacts, including their phone numbers, email addresses, and engagement details related to review requests. This tab allows you to manage contact records and track communication activity for email and SMS review requests.

## Why is the Contacts Tab Important?

Managing contacts centrally helps you:

- Keep communication records organized  
- Monitor review request history  
- Ensure contact information is complete and accurate  
- Use filters to segment audiences for review outreach  

## What’s Included in the Contacts Tab?

The Contacts tab includes:

- **Contact List**: A table of contact names, phone numbers, email addresses, and communication history  
- **Filters Panel**: Tools to create and apply filters based on contact attributes  
- **Columns Manager**: Customize which data columns are visible  
- **Bulk Tools**: Options for importing contacts and performing bulk actions  
- **Review Request Activity Columns**: Track the last email or SMS request sent, and any follow-up activity  

## How to Use the Contacts Tab

### View and Navigate Contacts

1. Open the `Contacts` tab in Reputation Management.  
2. Use the search bar to find a specific name, phone number, or email.  
3. Scroll through the list or change the page size using the dropdown at the bottom.

### Add a New Contact

1. Click the `Create contact` button in the top right corner.  
2. Enter the contact’s name, phone number, and email address.  
3. Click `Save`.

### Import Contacts in Bulk

1. Click the `Import` button.  
2. Upload a properly formatted CSV file.  
3. Map the fields (e.g., name, phone, email) and complete the import.

### Apply Filters

1. Click the `Filters` button on the left side.  
2. Use `+ Add filter` to filter by attributes like phone number, email, or activity.  
3. Apply multiple filters to refine your view.

### Customize Columns

1. Click the `Columns` button near the top right.  
2. Select or deselect fields to control which columns appear in the contact table.  
3. Save the view for quick access later.

## Review Request Activity

Each contact record includes these optional activity fields:

- `Last review request email sent`  
- `Last review request email activity`  
- `Last review request SMS sent`  
- `Last review request SMS activity`  

These fields remain empty unless a review request is sent to the contact through Reputation Management.

:::info
You can click the three-dot menu beside any contact to view, edit, or delete their details.
:::

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<details>
<summary>What types of filters can I use?</summary>

You can filter contacts by phone number, email, tags, and review request activity fields.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I remove a contact?</summary>

Click the three-dot menu beside the contact and select `Delete`.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I bulk upload contacts?</summary>

Yes. Use the `Import` button and upload a CSV file with the appropriate columns.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What happens if I import duplicate contacts?</summary>

Duplicate contacts are typically merged based on matching phone numbers or email addresses.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why are some review activity fields blank?</summary>

Activity fields remain blank unless a review request was sent to the contact from this tab.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I customize the table view?</summary>

Yes. Use the `Columns` button to show or hide fields.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Is there a way to view only contacts with missing emails?</summary>

Yes. Use the `Filters` panel to filter for contacts where the email field is empty.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I edit contact details?</summary>

Click the contact's name or the three-dot menu, then choose `Edit`.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I track if a review request was opened or clicked?</summary>

Yes. Use the `Last review request email activity` or `SMS activity` columns to view engagement.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does deleting a contact remove their history?</summary>

Yes. Deleting a contact removes all associated review request data from the system.
</details>
