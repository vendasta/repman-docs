---
title: "How can I remove specific reviews from the Review Widget?"
sidebar_label: "Removing reviews from Review Widget"
description: "Learn how to remove specific Google and Facebook reviews from displaying on the Review Widget"
---

To remove certain Google and Facebook reviews from displaying on the widget, go to **Reputation Management > Reviews > Manage Reviews > and select the "Share"** icon, which gives you the option to "Unpublish from Review widget":

![Screenshot showing how to unpublish reviews from the Review widget](./img/review-widget-unpublish.jpg)
