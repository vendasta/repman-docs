# Where can I find reviews left on 'My Listing' page?

My Listing Reviews are shown in **Reputation Management > Reviews > Manage Reviews.**

**Note:** If you want to publish them on the **My Listing** page, click on the **Share** icon beside the review > click on **Publish on My Listing.**
